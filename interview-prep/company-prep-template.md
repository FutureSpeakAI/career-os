## Red Flags to Watch For

- [ ] Vague success metrics (they do not know what they want this role to do)
- [ ] No hiring authority (you will lead "AI transformation" with no team and no budget)
- [ ] AI theater (they want someone to say "we have AI" without doing the organizational work)
- [ ] Committee-driven governance (everything requires 6 approvals; nothing ships)
- [ ] Single champion risk (one executive believes in AI; everyone else is waiting it out)
- [ ] Turnover signals (3rd person in this role in 2 years; Glassdoor reviews mention chaos)
- [ ] Comp mismatch (they posted a big range but will anchor at the bottom)
- [ ] Relocation bait-and-switch ("remote" that becomes "hybrid" that becomes "in-office")
