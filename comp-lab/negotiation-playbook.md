## Framework: The Confident Opener

> "Based on market data for this role and my experience with [key proof point],
> I'm targeting [RANGE]. I'm flexible on structure -- base, equity, bonus split
> can all work. What matters is the total package and the opportunity."

## Framework: Geographic Discount Pushback

> "I appreciate the transparency on comp bands. The roles I'm competitive for
> are output-based, not location-based. My [key achievement] didn't depend
> on my postal code."

## Framework: Below-Target Counter

> "I'm comparing with opportunities in the [higher range]. I'm drawn to
> [Company] specifically because of [concrete reason]. Can we explore
> [target number] with [creative structure: signing bonus, equity, review timeline]?"

## Framework: Competing Offer Leverage

> "I want to be transparent -- I have another offer at [range].
> I'd prefer to join [Company] because [reason]. Can we close
> the gap on [specific number]?"

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Always negotiate (companies expect it)
- Use specific numbers, not ranges
- Reference market data
- Express genuine enthusiasm alongside the ask

**Don't:**
- Apologize for negotiating
- Accept the first offer without discussion
- Share your current salary unless required by law
- Make ultimatums you're not prepared to follow through on
