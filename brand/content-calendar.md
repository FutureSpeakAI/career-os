# Content Calendar

> Track your LinkedIn, blog, and social content to build professional visibility during your job search.

## Content Strategy

| Day | Type | Topic | Hook | Proof Point | CTA |
|-----|------|-------|------|-------------|-----|
| Mon | Thought Leadership | [Topic] | [Opening line] | [Data/metric] | [Call to action] |
| Wed | Case Study | [Project/achievement] | [Story hook] | [Quantified result] | [Engagement ask] |
| Fri | Engagement | [Industry question] | [Provocative question] | [Your experience] | [Discussion prompt] |

## Content Types

- **Thought Leadership:** Original ideas backed by experience
- **Case Studies:** Specific projects with measurable outcomes
- **Engagement Posts:** Questions and discussions that build network
- **Product Updates:** Open source or portfolio project news

## Publishing Cadence

- Target: 2-3 posts per week during active job search
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10am local time
- Repurpose: Turn interview prep stories into content
